Hi, I'm Dr. Arielle Johnson

I’m a scientist at the MIT Media Lab. I used to be the scientist at a restaurant called noma in Copenhagen. I study & work with the biological systems that produce flavor to try to make the best food possible. I collaborate with chefs, engineers, bartenders, scientists, restaurants, cheesemakers, writers, coders, labs, ethnobotanists, designers, foragers, historians, and others on food and flavor. I want to dismantle the boundaries between the kitchen and the laboratory. See some of those projects here.
